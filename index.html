Keyboard Cat

Keyboard Cat is the nickname given to an orange tabby cat named Fatso. The original VHS footage of Fatso was recorded by performance artist Charlie Schmidt in 1984. He put the cat in a shirt and manipulated his paws to make the cat appear to be playing keyboard. On June 7th,2007, Schmidt uploaded the video to his Youtube channel, originally titled "Cool Cat".

Why should I care?

It's a fat cat called Fatso who plays piano. What are you doing with your life?

Grumpy Cat

Grumpy Cat is the nickname given to Tardar Sauce, a snowshoe cat that rose to online fame after several pictures of her annoyed facial expressions were posted to Reddit in late September 2012.
